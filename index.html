THE PROCESS

Zero Backend. Pure Browser.

01
🖼
DROP YOUR IMAGE
Any JPG, PNG, WEBP. Your file never leaves your device — it's read by a hidden canvas element. Zero server uploads.
🔬
PIXEL SAMPLING
The app reads your image pixel-by-pixel at the chosen resolution. Each sampled pixel becomes one voxel column — taller where the pixel is brighter.
🟫
VOXELS BUILT
Three.js stacks BoxGeometry cubes for each sampled point. Colors are mapped from the image — or remapped to a chosen palette.
📤
DOWNLOAD & SHARE
Screenshot the scene as PNG via renderer.domElement.toDataURL(). Share via Web Share API on mobile, or copy a generated link on desktop.
